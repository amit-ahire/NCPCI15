Rules For Conference

  • Conference is open for Undergraduate and Postgraduate Students.
  • Maximum of three authors per paper is allowed. Rs.100/- extra will be charged per extra member.
  • Paper should be as per the IEEE format which can be found on the official website.
    • www.IEEE.org
  • The paper should not exceed 6 pages including figures, texts, diagrams and tables.
  • The paper should be Unique ( Uniqueness should be at least 70% ).
  • Demand Draft for registration:
    Amount: Rs. 250/- (for CSI members)
    Rs. 300/- (for non-CSI members)
    In favor of:
    CSI VIIT Students Branch, payable at Pune.
  • Participants must email the soft copy of the full length paper to csiviit.ncpci15@gmail.com ON or BEFORE 4th March, 2015. This should include a duly filled registration form, scanned copy of the demand draft and CSI membership card (if applicable) as an attachment with the paper.
  • Selected participant, after receiving of the required documents, will get a Certificate of Participation.
  • Selected papers will be printed in the proceeding with ISBN number.
  • If all the required documents are not received on time, the papers would not be eligible for selection.
  • Selected papers will be intimated to email id from which paper was received. They should download and fill the copyright form from the Important Links Tab.
  • Authors of the best of the selected papers will be invited to present their papers and will get a certificate of presentation.
  • The selected students are expected to bring along their college identity cards and CSI membership cards (if applicable) along with them for the presentation.

    • Basic Presentation Rules

  • Total 20 minutes will be allotted. 15 minutes for presentation and thereafter questions from the judges and audience (if any) for a maximum of 5 minutes.
  • The timing system will proceed as follows:-
    1. A signal will be given at the beginning of the presentation.
    2. A warning signal will be given at the end of the 12th minute.
    3. A stop signal will be given at the end of 15th minute. Students should stop speaking when the stop signal is given. The judges will assess a penalty for continuing to speak beyond the 15 minutes time limit.

  • At the end of 5 minutes, questions will be stopped, and a few minutes will be spent in filling out the score and comment sheets for each presentation.
  • The following equipment will be available:-
    A Computer with latest version of presentation software, overhead projector, podium and a blackboard.

About the Conference

"National Conference and Project Competition for Students in Computer Engineering and Information Technology" is a annual national conference organized by the CSI Student Branch of Vishwakarma Institute of Information Technology, Pune. It aims to provide a platform for undergraduate students to express their views and share knowledge from different fields of technology. This year it will be held in the institute on 14th March, 2015. The conference will provide a chance for students to showcase their technical work and experimentation. It will encourage those students presenting their papers on their mini-projects, final year projects and any experimentation done by them. Even though it may not be state of the art, the student's own experimentation will be highly appreciated. The topics for the conference have been chosen based on the thrust areas of research in academia and industry.

About the Project Competition

The project competition, to be held in parallel with the National Conference on 14th March, 2015, is a supporting activity aiming to providing further exposure to the student's technical talents. The final year students can showcase their project which they have worked upon in the course of the year. An exhibition of the projects of all the selected candidates will be held and assessed by the guests of honor and industry professionals. Student groups can participate in both / either of the two events.

About the college

Chairman

Prof.Dr.(Mrs.)B.S.Karkare
Principle,Vishwakarma Institute of Information Technology

Established in 2002, Bansilal Ramnath Agarwal Charitable Trust's, Vishwakarma Institute of Information Technology, Pune is a prestigious technical education institute under Pune University. It is an ISO 9001-2008 Certified Institute. The Computer Engineering and Electronics and Telecommunication Programs are NBA accredited.

It runs undergraduate courses in Computer, Information Technology, Electronics, Electronics and Telecommunication, Mechanical and Civil. It also runs post-graduate course in Computer,Electronics and Telecommunication, Mechanical and Civil. The institution has always remained at the forefront in producing quality engineers who have brought laurels to the department and the institute.

CSI student's branch at VIIT has a current total strength of 476 students and has organized various guest lectures and workshops on current and emerging technologies.

About the student branch

CSI Students' Branch of VIIT was established in the institute in 2012, and since then it has been organising various different technical competitions and events under the ITSF and ACE which are the committees of IT and Computer Department respectively.
